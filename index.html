About Us

At Saniya Bridal Lounge, we believe in treating each bride with a personalized and luxurious experience that highlights her natural beauty. Located in Islamabad, our salon is renowned for its expertise in bridal makeup and hair styling.

With over 10 years of unparalleled service and a passion for beauty, we pride ourselves on delivering the highest standards of quality while ensuring that your journey from consultation to the final reveal is seamless and enjoyable.

Our professional and friendly team of stylists and beauticians specialize in creating stunningly flawless looks for any occasion, ensuring you shine at your most treasured events.
